What reforms are being asked for today?

In 2021, Black Spring CLE created a 5 year plan for police reforms in Cleveland. This document includes a few reforms to the judicial system, including:

1 Year

  • Establish a pretrial service center available to all, free at the point of service.
  • Establish and implement a community-run Jail Support organization to provide material, emotional, and legal support to incarcerated peoples.
3 Year
  • Abolish the practice of cash bail in perpetuity in Cuyahoga County with no "risk-determinance" replacement system.
5 Year
  • Decriminalize the possession or use of all drugs in Cuyahoga County, regardless of scheduled status
  • Expunge the records for drug-related possession charges in Cuyahoga County
