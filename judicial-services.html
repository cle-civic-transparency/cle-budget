JUDICIAL SERVICES?

Where does the money come from?

Funding for judicial services comes from the General Fund. The General Fund covers expenses for what we typically think a city does - Mayor, city council, police and more.

Specifically, the General Fund covers expenses for 11 departments which cover the following areas:

Mayor's Office
City Council
Public Safety
Fire
Emergency Responders
Judicial
Courts
Health Centers
Housing/Zoning
Recreation
Waste Collection
